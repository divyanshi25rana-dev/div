○◐ About

A researcher who still shows up to the room.

I'm currently an Assistant Professor of Psychology at Invertis University, Bareilly, having graduated with an M.A. in Psychology from Chaudhary Charan Singh University (CGPA 8.8, Gold Medal) and a BA (Hons.) in Psychology from Quantum University, Roorkee (CGPA 8.77, Chancellor's Medal). I qualified GATE in 2025 and 2026 and UGC-NET in December 2024 and June 2025.

My work sits at the overlap of clinical practice and academic research — psychometric assessment, behavioural intervention, and case management on one side; publishing, conference presentations, and curriculum design on the other. Before the classroom, I spent years on a volleyball court, which is probably why I still think of a good support system as something you build together, not something you're handed.
